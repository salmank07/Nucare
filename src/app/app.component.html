We Care at NuCare

About Us

NuCare Sdn Bhd is an investment holding company, engaged in the operation of community pharmacy dealing in the retail of pharmaceutical, healthcare, and personal care products. These include scheduled drugs, over-the-counter drugs, health supplements, personal care products, healthcare devices, healthy food, confectionery, beverages, and household products.

We are a start up in Penang, Malaysia, the first Pharmacy in the bustling new township of Bandar Cassia, Batu Kawan. We pride ourselves in providing holistic personal patient care. Our customers are ensured of a fresh new (Nu) approach to their pharmacy needs.

Our Vision

Reaching people and touching lives as a leading provider of valued medicines and services.

Our Mission

NuCare is passionately committed to providing diverse pharmacy and other health care products and services, emphasizing on service excellence, innovation and cost-effectiveness for the benefit of our patients.We aim to be an advocate of healthy lifestyles to the communities we serve and to create an amazing work environment offering career advancement opportunities for our dedicated team members.
